fix: prevent widget disappearance on desktop click and Win+D

- Refactored 'Always on top' toggle behavior to formally modify the island's window ownership (GWLP_HWNDPARENT) instead of relying on generic Z-order hacks.
- When 'Always on top' is disabled, the island is now structurally owned by 'Progman' (the Windows Desktop process).
- This inherently anchors the island directly above the wallpaper, completely preventing it from disappearing or being covered when the desktop is clicked.
- Because the island is now owned by the desktop, Windows natively exempts it from the 'Show Desktop' (Win+D) command, making it behave flawlessly as a true bottom-layer widget.

Fixes https://github.com/devcode90/Dynamic-Island-for-Windows/issues/16
