fix: resolve mouse wheel scroll delay and looping

- Reduced mouse wheel debounce timer from 300ms to 150ms for snappier response.
- Injected missing layout invalidation (g_layoutDirty = true) during tab changes (scroll and click) to guarantee instantaneous, zero-latency redraws instead of waiting for natural tick updates.
- Removed modulo wrapping (looping) from mouse wheel navigation so users reliably stop at the first or last tabs instead of unexpectedly wrapping around.

Fixes https://github.com/devcode90/Dynamic-Island-for-Windows/issues/15
